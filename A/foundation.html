Epos Education Rotterdam
Foundation

Primary school Het Epos falls under the foundation Epos Onderwijs Rotterdam. Stichting
Epos Onderwijs Rotterdam is a unique educational organization with core values of
happiness, growth, achievement, perseverance, curiosity and community. With a social
objective, and financed by private resources, we have a dream: we want to offer the
best possible education to children who need it most. We believe that children can
achieve much more with the right support.

The dot on the horizon for the EOR is to realize an integrated educational offer for all
children in the Rotterdam district, Zuidwijk. In addition to primary school Het Epos, EOR
consists of an after-school offer, Zuidwijs (weekend offer) and EOR is working on an
open preschool.

EOR Board

Stichting Epos Onderwijs Rotterdam has a board. This board is
closely involved in our projects on a voluntary basis and has overall
final responsibility for the projects, including Basisschool Het Epos
and Zuidwijs.
