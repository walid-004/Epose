Een School vol helden

Primary school Het Epos is a special school. It is a completely new school. If you choose us, you choose to become part of our community. Together we build the best possible education for our children. This is a journey of discovery.

Atmosphere at our school

Together we create a pleasant atmosphere in which your child feels at home. Peace, structure and involvement are important at our school. Your child develops self-confidence and discovers his or her talents. Our school is a place to learn. Making mistakes is part of it. That is how children learn.

At Het Epos, every child can be themselves and we accept each other. We believe it is important to treat differences between people, such as differences in religious beliefs, origins and orientation, with respect and openness. We expect this from our employees, students and parents or guardians [1] . We believe it is important to discuss themes in this area. Everyone is different, but we are all equal.

our education vision

The Six Valuse of The Epic

At primary school Het Epos, the quality of education is paramount. We have the ambition to be among the best schools in Rotterdam. We are committed to doing a little better every day. At Het Epos, we focus on six core values ​that are reflected in our education.

Community

At Het Epos we are responsible for each other and we help each other.



Perseverance

Not everything goes right the first time, but with perseverance we can achieve more than we think!



Performance

We have high ambitions and together we strive for high results.


Curiosity

Curiosity makes people want to learn. We challenge children to be curious every day.



Grow

With a focus on growth, we get the best out of ourselves and our children every day.




Happiness

Children learn best when they are also having fun!

What is an epic?

Hero story

An epic is a story about a hero. The hero often goes on a journey and overcomes adversity. During this journey, the hero discovers his or her talents and good qualities.

Values

Stories are used to pass values ​​from generation to generation. Community, curiosity, perseverance, growth, achievement and happiness are integral parts of an epic

Well-known stories

All cultures have their own heroic stories. Perhaps you know stories from the 1000 and 1 nights, or you know the epic of Gilgamesh, one of the oldest stories in the world.

Our children

Children experience their own heroic story. At Het Epos we give our children tools to do this successfully.
