The Six Valuse of The Epic

At primary school Het Epos, the quality of education is paramount. We have the ambition to be among the best schools in Rotterdam. We are committed to doing a little better every day. At Het Epos, we focus on six core values ​that are reflected in our education.

Community

At Het Epos we are responsible for each other and we help each other.



Perseverance

Not everything goes right the first time, but with perseverance we can achieve more than we think!



Performance

We have high ambitions and together we strive for high results.


Curiosity

Curiosity makes people want to learn. We challenge children to be curious every day.



Grow

With a focus on growth, we get the best out of ourselves and our children every day.




Happiness

Children learn best when they are also having fun!
